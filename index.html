About Us

History: Established in 1998, AM Coffee House was born out of a shared passion for exceptional coffee and a desire to create a welcoming space for the community. Founded by AM_ROVER, our journey began with a commitment to delivering a premium coffee experience, one cup at a time. Over the years, we've evolved into a cherished local destination, blending tradition with innovation in every sip.

Mission: At AM Coffee House, our mission is to elevate your mornings and redefine your coffee experience. We are dedicated to sourcing the finest coffee beans from around the world, ensuring a rich and flavorful brew in every cup. Our focus on using only the freshest ingredients reflects our commitment to quality and excellence. With a warm and inviting ambiance, we strive to create a haven where coffee enthusiasts and casual drinkers alike can gather, unwind, and savor the artistry of coffee craftsmanship. Menu

Espresso

A bold and concentrated coffee shot, crafted by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans

Iced Americano

A refreshing coffee beverage made by diluting espresso with cold water and ice. Smooth, crisp, and perfect

Latte

A velvety blend of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a thin layer of frothy milk. Creamy & balanced

Macchiato

An espresso "stained" with a dollop of frothy milk, balancing bold coffee with a touch of creamy sweetness

Lemonade
